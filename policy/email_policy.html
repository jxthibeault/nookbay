User Email Policy

The privacy and safety of our users is very important to us. That's why we handle your personal information as if it were our own. We do not currently require users to register with an email address when signing up for nookBay. However, some users may choose to provide one as means of contact or for use with future service enhancements. When you provide your email address to us, it is encrypted using a cryptographically secure, input-oriented algorithm before being saved to your account: without getting too complicated, that means even in the event of a data breach, bad guys need to know your email address from the get-go to unlock your data. Your email address is also unable to be read or accessed directly by any of our service administrators or moderators.
