What About Cookies?

To enable our system to recognize your browser or device and to authenticate your account as you browse nookBay, we use cookies and other identifiers. We may also use cookies to keep track of items you are interacting with, conduct internal research to improve our services, improve security, analyze the performance of our services, and conduct diagnostics on our end-user experience.

You can manage browser cookies through your browser settings. The 'Help' feature on most browsers will tell you how to prevent your browser from accepting new cookies, how to have the browser notify you when you receive a new cookie, how to block cookies, and when cookies will expire. Note that if you block all cookies on your browser, you may notice a significant drop in the quality of service we are able to provide to you.
